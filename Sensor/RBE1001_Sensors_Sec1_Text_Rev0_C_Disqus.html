Sensors

Introduction

An integral part of robots, the part that diffirentiates them from other machines are its sensors. Sensors act as a link between the robots physical setting and its programming, allowing it to make itelligent decisions based on the conditions. In this section you will learn about gyros, encoders, potentiometers, switches, and two different types of rangefinders (Ultrasonic and Infared). In doing so, you will also learn the basics of how they work and how to use them with the Arduino. Additionally this secion will cover how to use these sensors in properlly controlly your robot using these sensor's input, including basic control theory and I/O of the Arduino.
